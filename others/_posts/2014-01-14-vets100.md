<p>This dataset contains required veteran employment data by federal contractors/subcontractors. There are two tables V100DataDotGov and V100ADataDotGov. Both collections contain the past two years plus current filing cycle. V100DataDotGov are reports entered for contracts that were established prior to December 1, 2003 and greater than $25,000.00. V100AdataDotGov are reports entered for contracts that were established after December 1, 2003 and greater than $100,000.00. For additional information on data being reported review information at http://www.dol.gov/vets/vets-100.html#add.</p>
